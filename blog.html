President's Message

It has been my honor to host the vibrant, special, and enthusiastic cultural events of The Navrang Club, PSIT – “Show Your Talent” and “The Navrang Beats” in IGNITIA 2k24. I recall my journey as a complete fresher in PSIT – full of dreams and energy, eager to explore different aspects of college life, develop strong networks, and hone my interpersonal skills alongside my academic studies. With a passion for music, I used to play guitar in my leisure time. Then, I was introduced to the amazing cultural club of our college – The Navrang Club. I was completely mesmerized and driven towards the events the club had planned at the time and was keen to be a part of them.

Vaibhavi Didi (Our Ex-President ) was leading the Club at that time. I was truly inspired by the way she managed and planned out various and energetic events for all of us, making sure there were no inconveniences. She truly motivated me. I approached her, asking to be part of the club. She suggested I serve as a Club Volunteer and explained the process of joining something extraordinary. That day, a complete fresher, and today, I stand before you all as the President of The Navrang Club, PSIT – it has been quite a journey so far and a complete honor to me. It has been a roller-coaster ride. There were the initial days, when being part of and managing events seemed difficult, and also the days where post-event, we used to dance together to our favorite beats! Vaibhavi Didi has been a complete source of motivation for me! (Thank you so much Didi for being you! )

Mukesh Sir (The Navrang Club PSIT's Club Ambassador) was our primary source of guidance, advice, and support whenever we needed it. Without his vision for the club, it all looked impractical. Muskan, the Vice President of the club, was also a big support in my journey, helping me make the dream come true! My team - Akriti (Club Secretary), Ojasvee (Singing Head), Nishant (Social Media Head), Shashank (Treasurer), Rachit (Promotional Head), Rishi (Graphics Head), Vijay (Creative Head), and Usman (Technical Head) - was my biggest strength throughout my journey. The Navrang Club will always hold a special place in my heart!

This year, our annual techno-cultural fest ‘IGNITIA 2k24’ had the theme “Adhvanik,” presenting the memories, the moments of joy we shared going down memory lane, and the spark for the future! The Navrang Club, PSIT, along with our partner The Navrang Club, PSIT-CHE, decided to plan two events to engage the crowd and ensured an amazing time in IGNITIA! “Show Your Talent” provided a platform for students to showcase their incredible talents and rule the stage! “The Navrang Beats” offered a platform to present a theme-based performance and win the cheer of the audience. We are grateful for the energetic response from the PSITians, which made the events successful.

Signing Off! Prakhar Srivastava President, The Navrang Club PSIT
