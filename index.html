Testimonials

LinkedIn might say you'll find me navigating the professional landscape, but as the President of The Navrang Club, I am at the center of a community that revels in creativity. We weave stories through dance, ignite emotions with music, and bring to life a world of imagination. It's all about connecting with this incredible bunch of creative souls and fostering a space where artistic expression flows freely. Basically, it's all about the good vibes! The Navrang Club is not just a group, but a space where passions thrive.

President -Prakhar

the most remarkable aspects of the club is its ability to bridge divides and cultivate understanding among members of the college community. By showcasing the richness of various cultures, languages, and customs, the club promotes empathy, respect, and appreciation for diversity. It provides a platform for students to share their heritage, stories, and talents, fostering a sense of belonging and unity.

Vice- President-Muskan

This is a platform where students get to unleash their hidden talents and discover their passions and strengths. It's all about helping them find what truly excites them and where their natural abilities lie.

Social Media Head -Nishant

As President of The Navrang Club, I believed it was a vibrant cultural collage of diverse talents and passions. Our club served as a creative hub, fostering unity through a kaleidoscope of artistic expression, from dance and music to literature and cuisine. We celebrated the rich tapestry of cultures within our community, promoting inclusivity and understanding. Through our events and initiatives, we aimed to ignite curiosity, spark dialogue, and cultivate a sense of belonging among our members. The Navrang Club wasn't just a club; it was a colorful mosaic where every hue contributed to the beauty of our collective experience.

Ex-President-Vaibhavi

The Navrang Club is a consistently fostering a vibrant environment that celebrates creativity and cultural exchange through a diverse range of activities. This dynamic club allows students to explore their artistic talents, build strong connections with like-minded peers, and contribute to a rich tapestry of cultural appreciation on campus.

Club Ambassador -Mr Mukesh Dubey

The Navrang club is like a colorful mix of talents and culture. We all bring uniqueness to the table, making our club more vibrant and exciting. It's a fun place where we celebrate our differences and come together to have good time.

Technical Head-Mohd Usman

Navrang Club is a place where you can organize yourself to learn more about India’s Cultural Heritage. For teachers it is a tool to create awareness, develop respect and appreciation towards Indian arts. And also motivate students to undertake action-projects to conserve Indian Cultural traditions.

Secretary-Akriti

The Navrang Club is the only club for cultural festives in our college. The club provides the opportunity to students to showcase their inner talent on stage infront of huge audience in any cultural events (such as dancing, singing, art, open mic, dramas,etc.) Being a singing head, I personally love singing and this club provides me the place to do so. This club really has my heart.
